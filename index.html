Handcrafted Irish Traditional Instruments

Black Castle is a small Wicklow workshop focused on Irish traditional instruments and the details that matter: stable tuning, comfortable feel, and a sound that sits right in a session.

Banjos

The Black Castle banjo will be an ornate instrument resembling the vintage pre-war model, offering volume, sustain and true character.

Bodhrans

Carefully crafted for rich, resonant sound and easy playability.

Repairs & Setup

We offer repair and setup services for all types of instruments.

Our Story

Born from session nights at the Black Castle pub and music played across Arklow and Wicklow, we’re a small group of craftsmen and musicians working to put Wicklow firmly on the Irish traditional map.
